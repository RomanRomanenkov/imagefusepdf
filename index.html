FAQ

Answers before you install.

Do you compress or watermark my PDF?

Never. The export preserves your images cleanly with no watermarks or forced compression.

Can I capture images from the page I'm on?

Yes. Use the in-page picker to grab visuals from any site, then combine them with local files.

Which browsers are supported?

ImageFuse PDF is built for Chrome and Chromium-based browsers that support the Chrome Web Store.

Does it work offline?

Once installed, the extension can generate PDFs without an internet connection.

Privacy

Privacy policy

Local processing: All PDF creation happens locally in your browser. Images and generated PDFs are not transmitted to external servers.

No tracking: ImageFuse PDF does not collect analytics, personal data, or browsing history.

Permissions: The extension requests only the access required to capture images from the page and to save the generated PDF file.

Support: If you have privacy questions, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
